Ketchapp Games

When it comes to Android games, nobody can beat Ketchapp. It is a subsiduary of Ubisoft Company and is a publisher. Ketchapp publishes games made by some top GameDevelopers. Ketchapp is famous for its illustrative endless games which are very addictive. Ketchapp's games also recieve awards from Gamestop and this year it recieved award for Best Android Game Publisher Of The Year
